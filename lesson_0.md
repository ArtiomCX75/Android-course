Мой топ:
- Multi-cursor: `alt+J` (select text and press it to do magic :) )
- Duplicate current line or selection	`Control+D`/`Command+D`
- Project quick fix (show intention actions and quick fixes):	`Alt+Enter`/`Option+Enter`
